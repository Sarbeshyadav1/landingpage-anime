Anime..?

According to the Japanese concept of anime, this term is used to classify all types of animations, regardless of their origin (national or foreign). The origin of the word anime is uncertain. Some researchers consider it to be an abbreviation of the English word "animation," while others argue that it may have originated from the French word "animée" (meaning "animated"). Anime can encompass numerous different types, such as children's, horror, romance, comedy, adventure, erotic, science fiction, etc.

characteristcs of animes

The drawings of anime are characterized by the intense portrayal of the characters' feelings and emotions through their facial expressions. The large, vibrant, and colorful eyes, along with exaggerated facial expressions and onomatopoeias, are some of the most common features in anime.

Top studios

Mappa

MAPPA Co., Ltd. is a Japanese animation studio founded on June 14, 2011, by Masao Maruyama, the founder and former producer of Madhouse. "MAPPA" is an acronym for Maruyama Animation Produce Project Association.

Ufotable

Ufotable is a Japanese animation studio based in Suginami, Tokyo. Hikaru Kondou founded the studio in October 2000. The company's name originates from a UFO-shaped table that Kondou encountered while envisioning the studio.

Pierrot

Pierrot Co., Ltd. is a Japanese animation studio founded in May 1979 by former employees of Tatsunoko Production and Mushi Production. Pierrot is known for several popular anime series worldwide.

about
